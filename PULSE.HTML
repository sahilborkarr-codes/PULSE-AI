About PulseAI

PulseAI is an intelligent agentic AI platform that unifies social media analytics across LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, and X into one powerful dashboard.


Using IBM Granite Models, IBM Langflow, and IBM Orchestrate, PulseAI predicts trends, analyzes audience sentiment, tracks competitors, generates creative content, and recommends the best posting strategy in real time.

Core Features

📊 Social Listening

Monitor hashtags, mentions, comments, competitor activities, and trending conversations across LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook and X in one unified dashboard.

🤖 AI Content Generator

Generate captions, LinkedIn posts, tweets, Facebook posts, campaign ideas, hashtags and call-to-actions that match your brand voice.

😊 Sentiment Analysis

Understand how your audience feels using AI-powered positive, neutral and negative sentiment analysis with real-time recommendations.

📈 Trend Prediction

Forecast trending hashtags, viral topics, engagement patterns and the best posting schedule.

🏆 Competitor Intelligence

Track competitor campaigns, engagement rates, popular content, posting frequency and audience growth.

📅 Smart Scheduler

Receive AI recommendations for the best day, time and platform to publish your content.
